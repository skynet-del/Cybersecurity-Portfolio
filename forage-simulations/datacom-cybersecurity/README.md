# Datacom Cybersecurity Simulation

## Overview

This simulation focused on how Datacom’s cybersecurity team helps protect clients from cyber threats. The work involved investigating a simulated cyberattack, documenting findings, assessing risk, and recommending improvements to strengthen a client’s cybersecurity posture.

## What I Did

I investigated a simulated cyberattack and reviewed the situation from the perspective of a cybersecurity team supporting a client. I documented key findings, analyzed areas of concern, and organized recommendations that could help improve the client’s cybersecurity posture.

I also completed a risk assessment to identify potential weaknesses and connect cybersecurity issues to business impact. This helped strengthen my ability to document findings clearly and communicate security recommendations in a professional format.

## Key Takeaways

This simulation helped me understand how cybersecurity teams investigate incidents, assess risk, and communicate improvements to clients. It also reinforced the importance of organized documentation, practical recommendations, and business-focused security thinking.
