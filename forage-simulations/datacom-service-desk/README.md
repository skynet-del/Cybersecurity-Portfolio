# Datacom Service Desk Simulation

## Overview

This simulation focused on IT support and incident management as part of Datacom’s managed services team. The work involved reviewing service desk tickets, prioritizing incidents, identifying business impact, escalating issues appropriately, and communicating clearly with affected users.

* Service desk operations

* Ticket categorization
* Incident prioritization
* Escalation procedures
* Troubleshooting
* Network outage analysis
* User communication
* Customer service during technical issues

## Tools & Concepts

* ITIL processes

* Service disruption communication
* Managed services support

## What I Did

I reviewed a simulated service desk environment involving multiple client tickets and technical support issues. I practiced categorizing, logging, and prioritizing incidents based on impact and urgency.

The simulation included a network outage scenario where I analyzed the issue, considered the business impact, and determined appropriate response priorities. I also practiced writing clear and empathetic follow-up communications to affected users while maintaining professionalism during a service disruption.

## Key Takeaways

This simulation helped me understand how service desk teams support business continuity by prioritizing technical issues, escalating incidents properly, and communicating clearly with users. It also reinforced the importance of structured ticket management, documentation, and customer-focused communication in IT support roles.
