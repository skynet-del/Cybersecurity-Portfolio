This simulation focused on client service delivery, industry research, expert engagement, and developing actionable insights for a client project. The work involved analyzing the sneaker industry, creating expert profiles, and preparing a persuasive client pitch.

## What I Did

I conducted a value chain analysis of the sneaker industry and identified key stakeholders, market dynamics, and business factors that could support client decision-making. I also developed expert profiles and prepared a persuasive pitch to communicate findings clearly.

Although this simulation was not strictly cybersecurity-focused, it strengthened transferable skills that are valuable in cybersecurity consulting, risk communication, client-facing IT support, and business-aligned security work.

## Key Takeaways

This simulation helped me build research, analysis, and presentation skills. It reinforced the importance of understanding business context, communicating clearly with clients, and turning research into actionable recommendations.
