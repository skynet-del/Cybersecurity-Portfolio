# Forage Academy Customer Service Simulation

## Overview

This simulation focused on client service scenarios within a simulated financial institution. The work involved active listening, empathy, customer needs analysis, account access support, knowledge-base navigation, and decision-making in a client-facing environment.

## What I Did

I worked through realistic customer service scenarios involving client needs, account access issues, and support communication. I practiced using active listening and empathy to understand concerns, then used internal knowledge-base information to provide accurate and timely responses.

This simulation helped strengthen my ability to support users, communicate clearly, and make decisions in a client-facing environment.

## Key Takeaways

This simulation reinforced the importance of communication, patience, accuracy, and professionalism when supporting users. These skills are directly useful in IT support, service desk, cybersecurity support, and any role where technical problems affect real people.
