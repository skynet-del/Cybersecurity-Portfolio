# Mastercard Cybersecurity Simulation

## Overview

This simulation focused on security awareness and phishing threat identification as part of Mastercard’s Security Awareness Team. The work involved identifying potential security threats, analyzing business areas that needed stronger security training, and supporting recommendations to reduce risk.

- Business risk analysis
- User behavior risk reduction
- Security training recommendations
- Cybersecurity communication
- Analytical thinking

- Security awareness programs
- Business unit analysis
- Risk reduction strategies

## What I Did

I reviewed simulated cybersecurity scenarios involving phishing-related threats and identified areas where users or business teams may need stronger security awareness. I analyzed which parts of the organization could benefit from additional training and supported recommendations to improve security behavior.

The simulation helped me practice thinking about cybersecurity from both a technical and human perspective. It also showed how security awareness programs can reduce organizational risk by helping users recognize and respond to threats.

## Key Takeaways

This simulation reinforced the importance of phishing awareness, user education, and targeted security training. It helped me understand that cybersecurity is not only about tools and systems, but also about helping people make safer decisions.
